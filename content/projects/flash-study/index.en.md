Flash Study was a flash cards app that allowed students from all over the world to memorize information. The app was written in Objective-C and used Storyboards for the UI. Flash Study was featured as:

1. Canada's 19th top app in the education category
2. United States' 20th top app in the education category
3. New Zealand's top 60 grossing iPhone app
4. App of the day on the [Cocatech](https://cocatech.com.br/cast) podcast

**Check this project on [Github](https://github.com/lorenzopicoli/FlashStudy)**

---

Technologies: Objective-C, Storyboards
