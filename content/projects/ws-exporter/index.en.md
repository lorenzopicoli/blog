Wealthsimple is an investment/banking company used in North America, and I use it for many of my financial transactions. One of its major limitations is the lack of export options. To address this, I built this browser extension (supporting Google Chrome and Firefox) that automatically exports your transactions to CSV with a single click.

This project is currently active and I use it monthly as part of my [PTA](https://plaintextaccounting.org/) approach to keeping track of my finances.

**Check this project on [GitHub](https://github.com/lorenzopicoli/ws-exporter)**

**[Download this extension for Chrome](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/wealthsimple-exporter/kbgoacabffdbahjiheololcfgkhdambm)**

**[Download this extension for Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-CA/firefox/addon/ws-exporter/?utm_source=addons.mozilla.org&utm_medium=referral&utm_content=search)**
